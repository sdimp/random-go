Random Go!

What is this?

A bare-bones implementation of Go on randomly generated planar graphs.


What are the rules?

For the most part, the rules are identical to those of Go.

  • The number of liberties of a stone is equal to the number of unoccupied neighboring vertices.
  • A block of stones is captured when they have zero liberties.
  • The ko rule: you are not allowed to make a move that returns the game to the previous position.
  • There is no komi.
  • We follow Chinese scoring; you can manually select territory in case the scoring is janky.
